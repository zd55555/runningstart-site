Scenario 2 of 2

You find Mrs. Chen sitting alone by the window, crying quietly. She looks up when you approach.

"I just want to go home. I don't belong here."

How would you respond?

Key Takeaways

Remember

✓ Name the emotion. "That sounds frustrating" or "I can see you're upset" goes a long way.
✓ Ask, don't argue. "Tell me more" creates connection. Correcting facts creates conflict.
✓ Skip "don't worry." It rarely helps. Acknowledging the worry does.
✓ You can't fix everything. Sometimes just being present and listening is enough.
