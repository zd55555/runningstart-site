You Need a Budget

A new NBER report identifies 6.1 million American workers who are both highly exposed to AI and least equipped to adapt if their jobs get disrupted. Overwhelmingly clerical and administrative roles. Disproportionately women. What makes them vulnerable isn't just AI exposure. It's limited savings, narrow skill sets, and fewer local options to fall back on.

You need a budget. Regardless of who you're teaching, K to gray, that's one of two key messages I'd be driving home right now.

A budget doesn't stop disruption. But financial stability buys time. And time is what people need to retrain, pivot, or just make a decision without panic. Figuring out right now whether you spend more than you make is essential. If you're check to check, all the more reason. If you work with adults who are, even more so. There are ways to get support (e.g., dial 211 or visit 211.org).

The second thing I'd teach? Information literacy in the age of AI-powered disinformation.
